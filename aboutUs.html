Bhuvan Krishnamohan

Hi! My name is Bhuvan Krishnamohan. I am a passionate dog owner and I love working with dogs. We created the DHAR Organization to support the puppies that are struggling in puppy mills. Our mission is to end the cruelty of the puppy mills, so that they can get the lives that they deserve.
