What Do We Do?

Our Mission

At Dogs Help and Rescue (DHAR), our mission is to raise awareness about the cruel practice of puppy mills and advocate for the well-being of dogs who suffer in these inhumane conditions.

Puppy mills are commercial breeding operations that prioritize profit over the health and welfare of animals. These facilities often house dogs in overcrowded, unsanitary conditions without access to proper veterinary care, socialization, or a safe living environment.

Our goal is to educate the public about the harsh realities of puppy mills and promote responsible pet ownership. We strive to provide rescue and rehabilitation to dogs rescued from these mills, offering them a second chance at a loving, healthy life.

With the support of compassionate individuals like you, we are working to end the cycle of cruelty and give every dog the chance to live a life of happiness and freedom. Together, we can make a difference.
